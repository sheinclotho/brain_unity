## Rationale
The dynamics analysis pipeline is crucial for accurate simulations and predictions. By optimizing specific components, we aim to improve efficiency, accuracy, and reproducibility in our results.

1. **D2 Sampling & Quality Metrics**:  Integrate D2 sampling techniques, focusing on quality metrics to ensure data integrity.
2. **DMD Terminology Corrections**: Update terminology used in Dynamic Mode Decomposition (DMD) for consistency and clarity.
3. **Critical Slowing Down Integration**: Implement methods to analyze critical slowing down trends within the dynamics.
4. **Evidence Grading for Response Matrix vs FC**: Develop a grading system based on evidence for analyzing response matrices against functional connectivity.
5. **Metadata & Reproducibility**: Enhance metadata management and strategies for reproducibility across experiments.
6. **Rubric Configuration**: Define clear metrics and criteria for evaluation purposes.
